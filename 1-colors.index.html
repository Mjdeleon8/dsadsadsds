My favorite color is Cyan

A greenish-blue color which is one of the primary subtractive colors, complementary to red.

Cyan is a color rich in symbolism and associations. It embodies a sense of calm and tranquility,
making it ideal for evoking feelings of relaxation and peacefulness.

cyan is often linked with cleanliness and purity, making it a popular choice for products associated with hygiene and sanitation.
